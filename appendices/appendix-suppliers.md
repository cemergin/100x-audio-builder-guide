# Appendix B: Component Suppliers and Resources

> A categorized directory of suppliers for every type of component and material you'll need for audio building. Each entry includes the website, what they're best for, typical pricing tier, and shipping notes.

---

## 1. General Electronics Suppliers

These distributors carry the broadest range of electronic components — resistors, capacitors, semiconductors, ICs, connectors, and more.

### Mouser Electronics
- **Website:** [mouser.com](https://mouser.com)
- **Best for:** Prototyping and production. Huge selection (6.8M+ parts), accurate stock, fast US shipping.
- **Pricing tier:** Mid-range. Price breaks at 10, 25, 100, 500, 1000.
- **Shipping:** US warehouse (Mansfield, TX). Free over $50 in the US. Same-day shipping for orders before 8 PM CT.
- **Notes:** Parametric search is excellent. Authorized distributor for all major brands — no counterfeit risk. *(See: Ch 40)*

### Digi-Key Electronics
- **Website:** [digikey.com](https://digikey.com)
- **Best for:** Finding parts by specification. The best parametric search in the industry. 13M+ parts.
- **Pricing tier:** Mid-range. Nearly identical to Mouser.
- **Shipping:** US warehouse (Thief River Falls, MN). Free over $50 in the US.
- **Notes:** Slightly better search experience than Mouser for specification-driven searches. Also an authorized distributor. *(See: Ch 40)*

### LCSC Electronics
- **Website:** [lcsc.com](https://lcsc.com)
- **Best for:** Bulk orders at dramatically lower prices. 1/5 to 1/10 the cost of Mouser for many components. Integrated with JLCPCB for PCBA.
- **Pricing tier:** Budget. The cheapest major distributor.
- **Shipping:** Ships from Shenzhen, China. Standard: 1-2 weeks. Express (DHL/FedEx): 3-5 days, $15-25.
- **Notes:** Some datasheets only in Chinese. Carries both name-brand and budget parts — choose carefully for signal-path components. "Basic" parts have no surcharge for JLCPCB assembly; "Extended" parts add a small fee. *(See: Ch 40)*

### Tayda Electronics
- **Website:** [taydaelectronics.com](https://taydaelectronics.com)
- **Best for:** Guitar pedal components at the lowest prices. Resistors, caps, pots, jacks, switches, enclosures, knobs.
- **Pricing tier:** Budget. Pots $0.60-1.00, 3PDT switches $1.50-2.00, enclosures $3-7.
- **Shipping:** Ships from Thailand. Standard: 2-3 weeks. Express: 1-2 weeks. Shipping cost: $3-8.
- **Notes:** Limited selection compared to Mouser/Digi-Key — focused on what pedal builders need. Custom enclosure drilling and powder coating service available. *(See: Ch 40)*

### Newark / element14
- **Website:** [newark.com](https://newark.com)
- **Best for:** Alternative to Mouser/Digi-Key when they're out of stock. Strong in industrial and power components.
- **Pricing tier:** Mid-range.
- **Shipping:** US warehouse. Standard rates.
- **Notes:** Less popular for hobby/DIY but a reliable backup source.

### Arrow Electronics
- **Website:** [arrow.com](https://arrow.com)
- **Best for:** Free samples of ICs from major manufacturers. Many components ship free with no minimum.
- **Pricing tier:** Mid-range to high.
- **Shipping:** Free shipping on many items (no minimum).
- **Notes:** The free samples program is excellent for prototyping — you can often get 2-5 samples of an IC for free.

## 2. Audio-Specific Suppliers

Suppliers specializing in components for loudspeaker, amplifier, and audio system construction.

### Parts Express
- **Website:** [partsexpress.com](https://partsexpress.com)
- **Best for:** THE one-stop shop for speaker building. Drivers, crossover components, binding posts, damping material, port tubes, cabinet accessories, amplifier boards, and tools.
- **Pricing tier:** Competitive, especially on Dayton Audio (house brand). Frequent sales.
- **Shipping:** US warehouse (Springboro, OH). Free over $99. Excellent packaging for fragile items.
- **Notes:** Tech Talk forums are an excellent community resource. Sponsors speaker design competitions. Carries kits for beginners. *(See: Ch 40)*

### Madisound
- **Website:** [madisound.com](https://madisound.com)
- **Best for:** Premium speaker drivers — SEAS, Scan-Speak, SB Acoustics, Purifi, Dayton Audio Reference series. Also premium crossover components (Mundorf, Jantzen).
- **Pricing tier:** Premium (reflecting the premium drivers they carry).
- **Shipping:** US warehouse (Madison, WI). Standard shipping rates.
- **Notes:** Publishes recommended crossover designs for many drivers they sell. Smaller selection than Parts Express but more focused on high-end. *(See: Ch 40)*

### Meniscus Audio
- **Website:** [meniscusaudio.com](https://meniscusaudio.com)
- **Best for:** Crossover components. Audio-grade capacitors (Audyn, Mundorf, ClarityCap), inductors (Jantzen, Mundorf), and power resistors (Mills, Mundorf).
- **Pricing tier:** Mid to premium.
- **Shipping:** US warehouse. Standard rates.
- **Notes:** Also carries some drivers and publishes crossover designs. Good source for hard-to-find audiophile-grade crossover parts.

### Dayton Audio (via Parts Express)
- **Website:** [daytonaudio.com](https://daytonaudio.com) / [partsexpress.com](https://partsexpress.com)
- **Best for:** Value-oriented speaker drivers, measurement microphones (UMIK-1, UMIK-2), amplifier boards (DTA/DMA series), and DSP products (miniDSP partnership).
- **Pricing tier:** Budget to mid-range. Exceptional value at every price point.
- **Notes:** Dayton Audio is the Parts Express house brand. Their drivers from $10 to $100 compete with brand-name drivers costing 2-5x more.

## 3. Tubes and Tube Amplifier Parts

Suppliers specializing in vacuum tubes, transformers, and tube amp construction materials.

### Antique Electronic Supply (AES)
- **Website:** [tubesandmore.com](https://tubesandmore.com)
- **Best for:** Broadest selection of tube amp parts. Tubes (JJ, Electro-Harmonix, Tung-Sol, Mullard reissues), transformers (Hammond, ClassicTone, Mercury Magnetics), tube sockets, turret boards, eyelet boards, cloth-covered wire, tag strips.
- **Pricing tier:** Mid-range.
- **Shipping:** US warehouse (Tempe, AZ). Standard shipping.
- **Notes:** The largest dedicated tube amp parts supplier in the US. Excellent for both new builds and restorations. *(See: Ch 40)*

### Tube Depot
- **Website:** [tubedepot.com](https://tubedepot.com)
- **Best for:** Curated tube amp kits (Fender Tweed Deluxe, Marshall 18W, Vox AC15 clones). Also individual tubes and parts.
- **Pricing tier:** Mid-range.
- **Shipping:** US warehouse. Standard shipping.
- **Notes:** Kits include everything (transformers, components, chassis, hardware) and are an excellent way to build your first tube amp. *(See: Ch 40)*

### Mojotone
- **Website:** [mojotone.com](https://mojotone.com)
- **Best for:** Guitar amp kits, speakers (their own brand), transformers, chassis, tolex, grill cloth, and amp-specific hardware.
- **Pricing tier:** Mid-range to premium.
- **Shipping:** US warehouse (Burgaw, NC). Standard shipping.
- **Notes:** Strong focus on guitar amplifiers. Their custom-wound transformers are well-regarded.

### Weber Speakers / Weber VST
- **Website:** [webervst.com](https://webervst.com)
- **Best for:** Guitar speakers (their own designs, many vintage voicings), tube amp kits, transformers, and components.
- **Pricing tier:** Mid-range.
- **Shipping:** US warehouse. Standard shipping.
- **Notes:** Ted Weber's speaker designs are legendary in the guitar world. Good source for amp-building supplies.

### ClassicTone (Magnetic Components Inc)
- **Website:** Available through distributors (AES, Mojotone)
- **Best for:** Quality aftermarket transformers for classic amplifier circuits (Fender, Marshall, Vox clones).
- **Pricing tier:** Mid-range. Generally $40-120 per transformer.
- **Notes:** Lead times of 2-4 weeks are common.

### Mercury Magnetics
- **Website:** [mercurymagnetics.com](https://mercurymagnetics.com)
- **Best for:** Premium, hand-wound transformers. The gold standard for boutique tube amplifier builders.
- **Pricing tier:** Premium. $100-300+ per transformer.
- **Shipping:** Made to order. Lead time: 4-8 weeks.
- **Notes:** Expensive but widely considered the best aftermarket transformers available. Each is hand-wound and tested. *(See: Ch 40)*

## 4. Enclosures and Chassis

### Hammond Manufacturing
- **Website:** [hammondmfg.com](https://hammondmfg.com) (buy through Mouser, Digi-Key, or Tayda)
- **Best for:** Die-cast aluminum enclosures for guitar pedals (1590 series, 125B). Also rack-mount chassis and larger enclosures for amplifiers and other electronics.
- **Pricing tier:** Budget to mid-range. $5-12 per pedal enclosure.
- **Notes:** The de facto standard for guitar pedal enclosures. *(See: Ch 40)*

### Bud Industries
- **Website:** [budind.com](https://budind.com) (buy through Mouser, Digi-Key)
- **Best for:** Alternative to Hammond for general-purpose aluminum and steel enclosures. Wider range of shapes and sizes.
- **Pricing tier:** Budget to mid-range.
- **Notes:** Good for non-standard enclosure sizes that Hammond doesn't offer.

### Mammoth Electronics
- **Website:** [mammothelectronics.com](https://mammothelectronics.com)
- **Best for:** Pre-drilled and powder-coated enclosures specifically designed for popular pedal circuits. Also carries pedal components, hardware, and finished pedals.
- **Pricing tier:** Mid-range.
- **Shipping:** US warehouse. Standard shipping.
- **Notes:** Convenient one-stop shop for pedal builders. Their pre-drilled enclosures save significant time.

### Tayda Custom Enclosure Service
- **Website:** [taydaelectronics.com](https://taydaelectronics.com) (under "Custom Drill" section)
- **Best for:** Custom-drilled and powder-coated Hammond enclosures. Upload your drill template, choose colors, and receive ready-to-build enclosures.
- **Pricing tier:** Budget. $5-10 per drilled and coated enclosure.
- **Shipping:** Ships from Thailand with component orders.
- **Notes:** The most cost-effective way to get professionally finished enclosures. Upload your drill template as a PDF. 2-3 week lead time.

### Protocase
- **Website:** [protocase.com](https://protocase.com)
- **Best for:** Custom sheet metal and aluminum enclosures for amplifiers and professional equipment. CNC-cut, bent, and finished to your CAD specifications.
- **Pricing tier:** Premium. $100-500+ per custom enclosure depending on complexity.
- **Shipping:** Ships from Nova Scotia, Canada. 2-3 week lead time.
- **Notes:** Expensive but produces professional-grade custom enclosures. Good for small-run amplifier chassis.

## 5. Connectors

### Neutrik
- **Website:** [neutrik.com](https://neutrik.com) (buy through Mouser, Digi-Key, or B&H)
- **Best for:** THE industry standard for professional audio connectors. XLR, speakON, 1/4" jacks, combo connectors, powerCON.
- **Pricing tier:** Mid-range. $3-8 per connector.
- **Notes:** Accepted as the benchmark for quality and reliability. Using Neutrik connectors signals professional quality. *(See: Ch 40)*

### Switchcraft
- **Website:** [switchcraft.com](https://switchcraft.com) (buy through Mouser, Digi-Key)
- **Best for:** Professional-grade 1/4" jacks and XLR connectors. The Switchcraft 11 open-frame jack is a pedal building favorite.
- **Pricing tier:** Mid-range. $2-6 per connector.
- **Notes:** US-made. Slightly different mechanical design from Neutrik. Some builders prefer Switchcraft for pedals and Neutrik for rack/panel equipment.

### Amphenol
- **Website:** [amphenol.com](https://amphenol.com) (buy through Mouser, Digi-Key)
- **Best for:** Industrial and professional connectors. XLR, RCA, and specialized connectors.
- **Pricing tier:** Mid-range.
- **Notes:** Major OEM supplier. Less commonly used in DIY but widely used in commercial audio equipment.

### Rean (by Neutrik)
- **Website:** Available through Mouser, Digi-Key
- **Best for:** Budget alternative to Neutrik from the same parent company. 1/4" jacks, XLR, and RCA connectors at lower prices.
- **Pricing tier:** Budget. $1-3 per connector.
- **Notes:** Good quality for the price. A sensible choice for products where Neutrik pricing isn't justified.

## 6. PCB Manufacturers

### JLCPCB
- **Website:** [jlcpcb.com](https://jlcpcb.com)
- **Best for:** Cheapest PCBs. 5 boards (100x100mm, 2-layer) for $2. Excellent PCBA (assembly) service.
- **Pricing tier:** The cheapest.
- **Turnaround:** 1-3 days production + 5-10 days standard shipping from China.
- **Notes:** Green solder mask is cheapest. PCBA setup is $8, plus per-component costs. Integrated with LCSC for component sourcing. *(See: Ch 40)*

### PCBWay
- **Website:** [pcbway.com](https://pcbway.com)
- **Best for:** Specialty PCBs — heavy copper, rigid-flex, aluminum substrate, unusual board shapes. Good customer service with dedicated project managers.
- **Pricing tier:** Budget (slightly more than JLCPCB). $5 for 5 standard boards.
- **Turnaround:** 2-5 days production + 5-10 days shipping.
- **Notes:** Known for purple solder mask. Good PCBA service. Project manager reviews your files and contacts you about issues. *(See: Ch 40)*

### OSH Park
- **Website:** [oshpark.com](https://oshpark.com)
- **Best for:** US-based premium boards. Distinctive purple solder mask with gold (ENIG) finish. No minimum quantity — order exactly the number you need.
- **Pricing tier:** Premium. $5/sq in for 2-layer (3 copies). A pedal-size board: ~$30 for 3.
- **Turnaround:** ~12 days (manufacturing + US shipping).
- **Notes:** Higher quality finish than JLCPCB/PCBWay standard options. Best for prototypes where US shipping speed matters. *(See: Ch 40)*

### Aisler
- **Website:** [aisler.net](https://aisler.net)
- **Best for:** European-based PCB manufacturing. Good option for EU-based builders avoiding international shipping.
- **Pricing tier:** Mid-range.
- **Turnaround:** 6-8 business days within Europe.
- **Notes:** Made in Germany. Good quality. Integration with KiCad.

## 7. Speaker Drivers

### Dayton Audio
- **Website:** [daytonaudio.com](https://daytonaudio.com) (buy through Parts Express)
- **Best for:** Value-oriented drivers across all categories — woofers, tweeters, midranges, subwoofers, full-range, PA drivers. The RS (Reference Series) and ES (Esoteric Series) lines are particularly well-regarded.
- **Pricing tier:** Budget to mid-range. $10-100 per driver.
- **Notes:** Exceptional value. Published Thiele-Small parameters. Extensive community designs available. The default choice for DIY speaker builders starting out.

### SB Acoustics
- **Website:** [sbacoustics.com](https://sbacoustics.com) (buy through Madisound, Parts Express)
- **Best for:** Premium drivers with excellent measured performance. Satori line (high-end), SB line (mid-range). Strong in tweeters and midranges.
- **Pricing tier:** Mid-range to premium. $30-200 per driver.
- **Notes:** Indonesian-manufactured with excellent quality control. Published Thiele-Small parameters. Many award-winning DIY speaker designs use SB Acoustics drivers.

### SEAS
- **Website:** [seas.no](https://seas.no) (buy through Madisound)
- **Best for:** Premium drivers. Excel line (high-end, magnesium and aluminum cones), Prestige line (mid-range). Norwegian design and engineering.
- **Pricing tier:** Premium. $50-300 per driver.
- **Notes:** Used in many commercial high-end speakers. SEAS Excel drivers are among the best available.

### Scan-Speak
- **Website:** [scan-speak.dk](https://scan-speak.dk) (buy through Madisound)
- **Best for:** Ultra-premium drivers. Revelator, Discovery, and Ellipticor lines. Danish engineering.
- **Pricing tier:** Premium to ultra-premium. $80-500+ per driver.
- **Notes:** The aspirational driver brand. Revelator tweeters and midranges are legendary.

### Eminence
- **Website:** [eminence.com](https://eminence.com) (buy through Parts Express, Amazon)
- **Best for:** Guitar speakers and PA/pro audio drivers. US-made. Known for guitar amp speakers (Legend, Patriot, Red Coat series) and PA woofers.
- **Pricing tier:** Mid-range. $40-120 per guitar speaker; PA drivers $40-200.
- **Notes:** The largest US speaker manufacturer. Guitar speakers: the Delta 10A and Legend series are popular. PA drivers: robust, high-SPL designs.

### Celestion
- **Website:** [celestion.com](https://celestion.com) (buy through various distributors)
- **Best for:** Guitar speakers. The Vintage 30, Greenback, and G12M are industry standards.
- **Pricing tier:** Mid-range to premium. $60-150 per guitar speaker.
- **Notes:** British heritage. The default choice for many guitar amplifier builders.

### Peerless by Tymphany
- **Website:** Available through Parts Express, Madisound
- **Best for:** Mid-range to premium drivers with unique technologies. SDS (Symmetric Drive System) woofers are highly regarded.
- **Pricing tier:** Mid-range. $30-120 per driver.

### HiVi (Swan)
- **Website:** Available through Parts Express
- **Best for:** Budget to mid-range drivers, particularly planar/ribbon tweeters at accessible prices.
- **Pricing tier:** Budget to mid-range. $10-80 per driver.
- **Notes:** Chinese manufacturer with good quality control. Their ribbon tweeters are exceptional value.

## 8. Wood and Cabinet Supplies

### Local Lumber Yards
- **Best for:** MDF, plywood (Baltic birch, cabinet-grade), and dimensional lumber for speaker cabinets.
- **Pricing tier:** Varies by region. MDF: ~$40-50 per 4'x8' sheet. Baltic birch plywood: ~$60-100 per 5'x5' sheet.
- **Notes:** Always buy in person — inspect sheets for voids, warping, and damage. Most lumber yards will cut sheets to rough size for free or a small fee.

### Rockler
- **Website:** [rockler.com](https://rockler.com)
- **Best for:** Woodworking supplies — veneers, edge banding, specialty adhesives, finishing supplies, hardware (hinges, latches, threaded inserts).
- **Pricing tier:** Mid-range to premium.
- **Shipping:** US warehouse. Standard shipping.
- **Notes:** Good source for wood veneer sheets for speaker cabinet finishing. Also carries portable vacuum bags for veneer pressing.

### Woodcraft
- **Website:** [woodcraft.com](https://woodcraft.com)
- **Best for:** Similar to Rockler — woodworking tools, supplies, and materials. Good selection of finishing products (oil finishes, lacquers, wax).
- **Pricing tier:** Mid-range to premium.
- **Notes:** Physical stores in many US cities — useful for selecting materials in person.

### Home Depot / Lowes
- **Best for:** Budget MDF, basic plywood, and construction lumber. Also tools, adhesives, and basic hardware.
- **Pricing tier:** Budget.
- **Notes:** MDF quality is adequate for painted or wrapped cabinets. Plywood quality varies — inspect before buying. Good for quick, local sourcing.

## 9. Finishing Supplies

### Powder Coating Services (Local)
- **Best for:** Professional, durable finish on metal enclosures and chassis. More consistent than spray paint.
- **Pricing tier:** $5-15 per pedal enclosure, $15-40 per amp chassis (batch pricing).
- **Notes:** Search for local powder coating, auto body, or metal finishing shops. Many offer batch pricing for 10+ pieces.

### Tayda Electronics Powder Coating
- **Website:** [taydaelectronics.com](https://taydaelectronics.com) (part of custom enclosure service)
- **Best for:** Factory powder coating on Hammond enclosures ordered through Tayda. Cheapest option.
- **Pricing tier:** Budget. Included in custom enclosure pricing ($5-10 total per drilled + coated enclosure).

### Mohawk Finishing Products
- **Website:** [mohawk-finishing.com](https://mohawk-finishing.com)
- **Best for:** Professional wood finishing products — lacquers, stains, toners, fillers, and touch-up supplies.
- **Pricing tier:** Professional.
- **Notes:** Used by furniture manufacturers. Their aerosol lacquers are convenient for small cabinet runs.

### Vinyl Wrap Sources
- **Parts Express** carries self-adhesive vinyl in common colors/textures for speaker cabinet covering.
- **Metra / Install Bay** — Automotive vinyl wrap works well for speaker cabinets.
- **Oracal 651** — Sign vinyl, available from Amazon or sign supply shops. Good for smooth, matte, or gloss finishes.

### Tolex and Grill Cloth
- **Mojotone** — The best source for amp-covering tolex, grill cloth, piping, and corners.
- **Tube Depot / AES** — Also carry tolex and grill cloth.
- **Pricing tier:** $15-25 per yard of tolex; $10-20 per yard of grill cloth.

## 10. Test Equipment

### Siglent Technologies
- **Website:** [siglent.com](https://siglent.com)
- **Best for:** Oscilloscopes, spectrum analyzers, signal generators, power supplies. Good mid-range test equipment.
- **Pricing tier:** Mid-range. Oscilloscopes: $300-600.
- **Notes:** Good value competitor to Keysight/Tektronix at a fraction of the price.

### Rigol Technologies
- **Website:** [rigol.com](https://rigol.com)
- **Best for:** The DS1054Z oscilloscope ($350) — the most popular oscilloscope among hobbyists and small-shop builders. 4 channels, 50 MHz (hackable to 100 MHz).
- **Pricing tier:** Budget to mid-range.
- **Notes:** The DS1054Z is the default recommendation for anyone buying their first scope. *(See: Ch 4)*

### miniDSP
- **Website:** [minidsp.com](https://minidsp.com)
- **Best for:** UMIK-1 and UMIK-2 calibrated USB measurement microphones for speaker and room measurement with REW. Also DSP boards for active crossovers (miniDSP 2x4 HD, SHD).
- **Pricing tier:** Mid-range. UMIK-1: ~$80. UMIK-2: ~$150. DSP boards: $100-600.
- **Notes:** The UMIK-1 + REW (free software) is the standard measurement setup for DIY speaker builders. *(See: Ch 15)*

### Fluke
- **Website:** [fluke.com](https://fluke.com)
- **Best for:** Multimeters. The Fluke 117 ($200) is the gold standard for a general-purpose multimeter. True RMS, auto-ranging, reliable.
- **Pricing tier:** Premium.
- **Notes:** More expensive than alternatives but built to last decades. A Fluke multimeter is a lifetime purchase.

### Budget Alternatives
- **Uni-T** multimeters — $20-50 range, adequate for hobby use.
- **Hantek** USB oscilloscopes — $70-150, good for basic work if a benchtop scope is too expensive.
- **FY6900** function generators — $60-100, capable dual-channel signal generators.

## 11. Miscellaneous Suppliers

### McMaster-Carr
- **Website:** [mcmaster.com](https://mcmaster.com)
- **Best for:** Mechanical hardware — screws, standoffs, brackets, rubber feet, adhesives, thermal compounds, grommets, cable management. If it's a physical thing that isn't electronic, McMaster has it.
- **Pricing tier:** Mid-range (premium for the selection and speed).
- **Shipping:** Same-day shipping from multiple US warehouses.
- **Notes:** Their website and CAD download system is legendary for its efficiency. *(See: Ch 40)*

### Uline
- **Website:** [uline.com](https://uline.com)
- **Best for:** Shipping supplies — boxes, foam inserts, bubble wrap, packing peanuts, tape, poly bags, labels.
- **Pricing tier:** Mid-range (best pricing at volume).
- **Shipping:** Same-day from US warehouses.
- **Notes:** The Amazon of packaging supplies. Useful when your packaging needs exceed what Amazon offers.

### Small Bear Electronics
- **Website:** [smallbear-electronics.mybigcommerce.com](https://smallbear-electronics.mybigcommerce.com)
- **Best for:** Specialty pedal components — matched transistor sets for fuzz pedals, rare/vintage ICs, germanium diodes, specific potentiometer tapers.
- **Pricing tier:** Premium (reflecting the selection and matching work).
- **Notes:** The go-to source for matched germanium transistors for Fuzz Face builds. Steve at Small Bear tests and matches every transistor pair.

### Stew-Mac (Stewart-MacDonald)
- **Website:** [stewmac.com](https://stewmac.com)
- **Best for:** Guitar and amp building tools and supplies — pickup winding supplies, shielding materials, amp grille cloth, cabinet hardware.
- **Pricing tier:** Mid-range to premium.
- **Notes:** Originally a luthier supply company. Good crossover products for amp and pedal builders.

### Amazon
- **Website:** [amazon.com](https://amazon.com)
- **Best for:** Tools, non-critical supplies (solder wick, flux, heat-shrink, cable ties, storage bins, LEDs), commodity wire.
- **Pricing tier:** Varies. Often cheapest for tools and supplies.
- **Notes:** Avoid for precision components, active components, tubes, and branded audio connectors (counterfeit risk). *(See: Ch 40)*

### AliExpress
- **Website:** [aliexpress.com](https://aliexpress.com)
- **Best for:** Bulk commodity components, tools, supplies, and non-critical hardware at rock-bottom prices.
- **Pricing tier:** The cheapest option for most things.
- **Shipping:** 2-6 weeks from China (standard). Express options available.
- **Notes:** Significant counterfeit risk for branded components and semiconductors. Use only for items where provenance doesn't matter. *(See: Ch 40)*
